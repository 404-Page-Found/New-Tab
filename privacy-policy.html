Privacy Policy

New Tab extension

This extension is designed to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

Data stored locally

The extension stores your preferences and content locally in your browser, including theme, language, background choices, app layout, todo items, onboarding progress, and AI conversation history. This data is saved only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Search

When you search from the new tab page, your query is passed to your browser's default search provider through Chrome's search API. The extension does not maintain its own search index or store your search queries on a remote service.

AI features

If you use the AI assistant or related features, the prompts you enter and any API credentials you configure may be sent directly to the third-party service you selected in order to provide the requested response. Those services process the data according to their own privacy policies.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data or share it with advertisers.

Retention

You can clear the stored data by removing the extension or clearing the browser data associated with it.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer through the project repository or the support channel linked with the extension listing.
